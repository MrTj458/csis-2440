Free Hometown Shipping!

If your shipping address is in our hometown of Redmond, Washington we offer to deliver your order personally and for free so you will have no shipping charges! If your shipping address is in one of several adjacent areas then we will still be happy to personally deliver your order for a very low fee. See our policies page for a list of qualifying zip codes.

Flat Rate Domestic Ground Shipping!

Shipping for all ground orders to anywhere in the continental United States up to $150 costs only $10. Shipping for orders over $150 costs only $5! See our policies page for details.
